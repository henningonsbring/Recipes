# Chili mayonnaise
## Ingredients
- 2x egg yolk
- 0.75 tsk dijon mustard
- 0.5 msk freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 0.5 krm white pepper
- 0.25 tsk salt
- 1.5 dl cooking oil
- 2 tsk sambal oelek
## Procedure
1. Mix egg yolk, dijon mustard, lemon juice, white pepper, and salt
2. Add cooking oil in a thin stream while beating the mixture
3. Add sambal oelek and stir until homogenous
