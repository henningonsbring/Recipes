# Dai Wei Niu Rou (傣味牛肉)
## Ingredients
### Marinade
- 200 g sirloin steak
- 1 tsk soy sauce
- 1 tsk baking powder
- 1 tsk potato starch
- 2 krm white pepper
- 2 krm black pepper
- 2 krm salt
### Cucumber
- 1x cucumber
- 0.5 tsk salt
### Mushrooms
- 200 g enoki mushrooms
### Remaining part
- 0.5x garlic
- 6x small fresh red chili
- 20 g coriander
## Procedure
### Marinade
1. Cut the sirloin steak in thin slices
2. Add soy sauce, salt, white pepper, black pepper, potato starch, and baking powder
3. Mix the meat with the marinade components, and let rest in the fridge
### Cucumber
4. Put cucumber in plastic bag and beat it with a rolling pin
5. Break, or cut, the cucumber into pieces, then put into a plate
6. Add salt to the plate and mix
### Mushrooms
7. Remove bottom from the mushrooms, then wash them
8. Put mushrooms in boiling water for 2 minutes
9. Rinse mushrooms with cold water
### Remaining part
10. Chop the garlic into small pieces
11. Chop the chili into 0.5 cm pieces and mix them with the garlic in a bowl
12. Steam beef slices for 3 minutes
13. Mix the beef, mushroom, cucumber, garlic, and chili
14. Add coriander on top
