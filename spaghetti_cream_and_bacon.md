# Spaghetti cream and bacon
Adapted from: https://www.arla.se/recept/pasta-carbonara-med-syrad-gradde/
## Ingredients
- 280 g bacon
- 3x garlic cloves
- 3 dl cultured cream (cream that has been fermented with lactic acid bacteria)
- 2x egg yolk
- Black pepper
- Butter (to fry)
- 50 g parmesan
## Procedure
1. Boil the spaghetti
2. Chop the garlic finely
3. Cut the bacon into thin strips
4. Heat butter in a pan
5. Fry bacon and garlic in the pan
6. Add half of the cultured cream to the pan and let simmer for 2 min
7. Add black pepper
8. Mix the remaining cream with egg yolk
9. Add the cream and egg yolk mix to the pan
10. Add the spaghetti to the pan and mix everything
11. Serve with parmesan on top
