# Sausage Stroganoff
Adapted from: https://www.arla.se/recept/korv-stroganoff-med-potatismos/
## Ingredients
- 300 g falukorv
- 1x yellow onion
- 2 msk tomato paste
- 1 tsk corn starch
- 3 dl cultured cream (cream that has been fermented with lactic acid bacteria)
- 1 tsk dijon mustard
- Butter (to fry)
- Rapeseed oil (to fry)
## Procedure
1. Cut the falukorv into strips
2. Chop the onion
3. Heat butter and rapeseed oil in a pan
4. Fry the falukorv, onion, and tomato paste together in the pan
5. Add 2.5 dl of the cream to the pan
6. Mix the corn starch with remaining cream
7. Add the corn starch and cream mix to the pan
8. Add the dijon mustard
9. Let content in the pan simmer over low heat for 2-3 minutes
## Serving Suggestions
Mashed potatoes: Boil 1 kg potatoes, mash, add 2.25 dl milk, 50 g butter, 0.75 tsk salt, 2 krm white pepper, 1 krm sugar
