# Christmas porridge
Adapted from: https://www.juligen.se/lyxig-tomtegrot/
## Ingredients
- 2 dl porridge rice
- 2-3 msk butter (approximately 35 g)
- 3 dl water
- 0.25 tsk salt
- 2.5 dl cream
- 5.5 dl milk
- 2x whole cinnamon sticks
## Procedure
1. Fry the porridge rice lightly in butter for a minute
2. Add water and the salt
3. Boil for 5-10 minutes until the porridge is thick
4. Add the cream, then stir
5. Add the milk, little at a time while stirring
6. Add the cinnamon
7. Boil at low temperature under lid for about 40 minutes, stirring occasionally
8. Taste and add more salt if needed
9. Remove the cinnamon and serve
