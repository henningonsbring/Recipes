# Creamed macaroni
Adapted from: https://www.scan.se/recept/2338/stekt-falukorv-med-stuvade-makaroner/
## Ingredients
- 4 dl macaroni
- 2 msk butter
- 1.5 msk flour
- 2.5 dl cooking cream
- 2.5 dl milk
- 1 krm salt
- 1 krm white pepper
## Procedure
1. Boil the macaroni according to instructions
2. Melt the butter in a pan or pot
3. Mix the melted butter with the flour
4. Add the cream and the milk
5. Bring the cream, milk, and flour mixture to a gentle boil, stirring continuously until it thickens
6. Add salt and white pepper
7. Add the boiled macaroni to the cream mix
