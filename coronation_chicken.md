# Coronation chicken
## Ingredients
### Chicken
- 600 g chicken
- 2x bay leafs
- 8x black peppercorns
- Salt
### Sauce
- 500 g cooked chicken
- 150 ml mayonnaise
- 2-3 msk creme fraiche
- 2 tsk curry powder
- 1 msk mango chutney
- 1 tsk lemon juice
- Salt
- Black pepper
- 1 msk butter
- 1x celery stalk
- 10 g fresh parsely (about 2 msk chopped)
## Procedure
### Chicken
1. Place chicken in a pan
2. Add bay leafs, pepper corns, salt
3. Pour cold water to cover the chicken
4. Bring gently to a bare simmer (do not boil)
5. Simmer for around 12-15 min (breasts) or 15-18 min (thighs)
6. Turn off the heat, cover, and let sit 5 minutes
7. Remove chicken and let cool, then dice or shred
### Sauce
8. Heat the butter gently in a small pan. Add curry powder and warm until fragrant (about 30 seconds)
9. Remove from heat and let cool
10. In a bowl combine all the sauce ingredients (except celery and parsely)
11. Add the chicken, celery, and parsely
12. Adjust seasoning if needed, then serve
